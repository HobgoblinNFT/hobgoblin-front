    MEET THE Hobgoblin

    Hobgoblin is a collection of 5,555 NFTs hopping about and banging on the Ethereum blockchain. (Ever wondered where "going at it like Hobgoblin originated? Its from us, the Hobgoblin fam.)

    Each Hobgoblin features 300+ trait variations, with all having something in common: a love of 24/7 partying and turning up. You can't miss it if you want a cute Hobgoblin!

    They may be frisky, but the Hobgoblin are helping us trailblaze the CC0 trend (aka open-source branding). Were talking extension projects, free NFT education, good vibes, and tons more.

    Join Hobgoblin and help us bridge the gap between Web3 and the physical world. Were all about the community here, and we cant wait to welcome our newest member: YOU!
